==================================================
Purchase behavior attributes
==================================================

.. grid-purchase-behaviors-about-start

Build audiences around purchase activities, such as first orders, repeat orders, and who has or has not purchased.

.. grid-purchase-behaviors-about-end

.. grid-purchase-behaviors-grid-start

.. grid:: 1 1 2 2
   :gutter: 2
   :padding: 0
   :class-row: surface

   .. grid-item-card:: First purchase?
      :link-type: doc
      :link: attribute_purchase_behavior_first_purchase

      Returns a list of customers who made their first purchase during your chosen date range.


   .. grid-item-card:: Has not purchased
      :link-type: doc
      :link: attribute_purchase_behavior_has_not_purchased

      Identifies all customers who did not make a purchase during the chosen date range.


   .. grid-item-card:: Has purchased
      :link-type: doc
      :link: attribute_purchase_behavior_has_purchased

      Returns orders that meet a defined threshold and occurred during the chosen date range.


   .. grid-item-card:: Most frequently ordered
      :link-type: doc
      :link: attribute_purchase_behavior_most_frequent_order

      Returns the products that customers ordered most frequently during the chosen date range.


   .. grid-item-card:: Repeat purchase
      :link-type: doc
      :link: attribute_purchase_behavior_repeat_purchase

      Returns a list of customers who have made a repeat purchase during the chosen date range.


   .. grid-item-card:: Total value of orders
      :link-type: doc
      :link: attribute_purchase_behavior_total_value_of_orders

      All revenue for all purchased items across all orders that match a value and a time period.
