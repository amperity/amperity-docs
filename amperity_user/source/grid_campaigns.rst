==================================================
Campaigns
==================================================

.. grid-campaigns-about-start

Use campaigns to build the audiences to which you want to send messages or offers. Configure campaigns to send these audiences to downstream marketing tools, such as email service providers, SMS providers, paid media, digital advertising, and personalization.
